How to Use RiskLab

Position Size Calculator

Enter your account balance, risk percentage, entry price, and stop loss to calculate your optimal position size. The calculator ensures you only risk your specified percentage per trade.

Formula

Position Size = Risk Amount / Stop Distance
Risk Amount = Account Balance x Risk %
Stop Distance = |Entry Price - Stop Loss|

Risk/Reward Ratio

Add a take profit price to see your R:R ratio. A 1:2 ratio means you profit $2 for every $1 risked. Professional traders aim for at least 1:1.5 or higher.

Liquidation Price

When using leverage, the calculator estimates your liquidation price using a simplified isolated-margin formula. Always verify with your exchange before trading.

Portfolio Mode

Add multiple positions to track your total portfolio risk. This helps ensure you don't exceed your maximum account risk across all open trades.
